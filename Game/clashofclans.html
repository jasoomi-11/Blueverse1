About Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans is a free-to-play strategy and base-building game developed and published by Supercell for Android and iOS devices. Since its release, it has become one of the most popular mobile games in the world, combining real-time strategy, resource management, and online multiplayer warfare.

In Clash of Clans, players build and upgrade their villages, train powerful troops, and attack other players to earn Gold, Elixir, and Dark Elixir. These resources are used to strengthen defenses, unlock new technologies, and improve your army‚Äôs combat potential.

The game features a wide range of troops, heroes, and spells that allow for endless strategic possibilities. Players can join or create Clans to participate in Clan Wars, Clan Games, and Clan War Leagues, where teamwork and coordination play a major role in victory.

Supercell continuously updates the game with new troops, defensive structures, events, and seasonal challenges. Each update adds depth and new goals for players, ensuring long-term engagement and competitive gameplay.

With its vibrant art style, tactical gameplay, and thriving online community, Clash of Clans remains a cornerstone of mobile strategy gaming. Whether you‚Äôre a casual builder or a competitive warrior, Clash of Clans offers endless fun and strategic depth for all players.
