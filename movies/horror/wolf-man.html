Story / Plot

Wolf Man (2025) follows a desperate family trapped inside their isolated farmhouse during a terrifying full-moon night. After a string of brutal attacks in their rural community, fear spreads as locals believe a deadly creature is lurking in the woods ‚Äî and it may already be much closer than anyone realizes.

When a severely injured man appears at their doorstep begging for help, the family reluctantly brings him inside. But as the night unfolds, they soon discover that the stranger is hiding a horrifying secret. His condition begins to deteriorate in violent and unpredictable ways, forcing the family to confront the possibility that he may not be human anymore.

As the full moon rises, the tension escalates. Trapped with no way to call for help, they must survive the night while a deadly transformation takes place inside their own home. The film blends claustrophobic suspense with emotional drama as the characters struggle not only against the creature but against distrust, fear, and their own unraveling sanity.

Directed by Leigh Whannell, Wolf Man delivers a modern, atmospheric reimagining of the classic Universal Monster tale. With a focus on realism, paranoia, and gritty survival horror, the film uses practical effects, dark lighting, and raw performances to craft a chilling slow-burn thriller.
